    About the Group

    Data Processing and Intelligent Computing Group is a research group at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women. It is actively involved in research and imparting advanced training through seminars, workshops and semester long courses in the fields of Database Management Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Information Retrieval, Fuzzy Logic, Natural Language Processing and Data Mining.

    More on the Group

    The group has 5 faculty members, over 20 research students, and around 30 undergraduate students working on different areas and projects under the leadership of Dr. Devendra K. Tayal. Several top researchers in the field have visited the University to conduct seminars and interact with the group.
